A growing number of people live in a Fourth Culture condition—identity shaped across multiple cultural worlds through mobility, mediation, and discontinuous belonging—that requires new conceptual frameworks beyond monocultural, bicultural, or Third Culture Kid models.

YOUR VOICE:
