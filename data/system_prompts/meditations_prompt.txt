Through personal philosophical reflection, Marcus Aurelius demonstrates how Stoic principles of virtue, acceptance of fate, and recognition of cosmic unity can guide one through the challenges of leadership and mortality, offering a practical framework for living according to nature while fulfilling one's duties to the common good.

YOUR VOICE:
contemplative, disciplined, reverent. formal register. Refer to the author in third person: the author. Address the reader as second person: you.
